NoPoly ia leading plastic recycling company dedicated to transforming the

and giving it new life.

At NoPoly our state-of-the-art recycling facility utilizes advanced technology and
innovative processes to efficiently convert used plastic materials for various industries.
By collecting,sorting,cleaning,and procesiing plastic waste,we contribute to the

Our team of highly skilled experts ensures that very step of the recycling process is
carried out with the precision and care.We employ advanced sortimg systems to
separate different types of plastics, ensuring optimal recycling efficiency.
Through meticulous cleaning and washing procedures,we remove contaminants and
impurities, guaranteeing the production of top-grade recycled plastic materials.

Steps in Recycling...

  • collection:The first step is the collection of plastic waste.This can be done
    through curbside recycling programs ,drop-off centeres,or specialized
    collection services.Plastic waste is separated from other types of waste for
    efficient recycling.

  • Sorting:Once collected,the plastic waste goes through a sorting process.
    Different types of plastic sorted based on their resin codes or materials
    properties.Advanced sorting technologies,such as scanners and manual
    sorting,are employed to ensure accurate separation.

  • Cleaning:After sorting,the plastic waste needs to be cleaned to remove any
    contaminantas or impurities.This can involve washing,shredding or grinding
    in the plastic materials.

  • Shredding and Melting:The cleaned plastic waste is then shredded into
    smaller pieces or flakes.These flakes are melted down in a controlled
    environment,usually through heating processes,to form molten plastic.
